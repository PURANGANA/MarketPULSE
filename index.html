About MarketPulse

MarketPulse is a market intelligence platform that brings together live pricing, predictive analytics, and portfolio tracking in a single, focused dashboard. It is built to make market data easier to read and act on.

Market Analytics

Live price feeds combined with a forecasting layer that estimates likely near-term price movement for each stock you follow.

Fast & Responsive

Built with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Chart.js, with a responsive interface designed to work well on both desktop and mobile.

Built Around You

Personal watchlists and a favorites-based dashboard let you track only the stocks that matter to your portfolio.

Academic Foundation

Developed as a final-year software engineering project, demonstrating applied web development, API integration, and forecasting concepts.

Our Services

A focused set of tools designed to simplify how you research stocks and track your portfolio.

Live Stock Search

Look up major listed stocks and view their current market price alongside a next-day price forecast.

Market News Feed

A continuously updating feed of financial headlines and market-moving developments, sourced live from the market.

Portfolio Watchlist

Save stocks to a personal favorites list for quick, ongoing monitoring from your dashboard.

Predictive Insights

Directional forecasts, labeled as bullish, bearish, or neutral, to support faster decision-making.

Visual Dashboard

An at-a-glance chart summarizing how your watchlist is trending across rising, falling, and neutral positions.

Live Market Status

Real-time indication of whether the market is currently open or closed, based on official trading hours.

Market News

Live financial headlines, refreshed automatically. If live data is temporarily unavailable, a recent cached summary is shown instead.
